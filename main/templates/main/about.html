Our Heritage Story

Founded on passion for automotive excellence and preservation of classic automobiles for future generations.

Preserving Automotive Heritage Since 1999

Barclay Heritage was founded with a simple mission: to preserve the rich history of classic automobiles and share the passion for vintage cars with enthusiasts worldwide.

Our journey began over two decades ago when our founder, James Barclay, recognized the need for professional heritage services in the classic car community. What started as a small collection has grown into one of the most respected heritage preservation companies in the industry.

Today, we continue to uphold the highest standards in automotive preservation, restoration, and authentication, ensuring that these mechanical works of art continue to inspire future generations.
